2-12mm Thickness Range

Product Overview

Our solid polycarbonate sheets are crystal-clear panels that combine glass-like transparency with extraordinary impact resistance. At 250 times stronger than glass and half its weight, they are the ideal choice for demanding glazing applications.

All sheets feature co-extruded UV protection on both sides, ensuring long-term resistance to yellowing and weathering. This dual-side protection allows installation without worrying about which side faces the sun.

Available in a wide range of colors including clear, bronze, grey, green, blue, and opal. Custom sizes available on request with maximum width of 2050mm.

Key Strengths

Why choose our solid polycarbonate sheets

250x Impact Resistance

Virtually unbreakable — 250 times stronger than glass. Ideal for security glazing, machine guards, and vandal-resistant applications.

Crystal Clear

Up to 90% light transmission for clear sheets — virtually identical to glass. Perfect optical clarity for architectural glazing.

UV Protection Both Sides

Co-extruded UV protective layer on both sides eliminates installation errors and ensures maximum durability from any angle.

Fire Rated

EuroClass B-s1,d0 fire classification. Self-extinguishing material suitable for public buildings and commercial projects.

Lightweight

Half the weight of glass at the same thickness. Reduces structural support requirements and simplifies installation.

Cold Bendable

Can be cold-bent on site for curved applications. Minimum bending radius equals 150 times the sheet thickness.
