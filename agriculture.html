Agriculture & Greenhouses

Polycarbonate glazing solutions that create optimal growing conditions with superior light diffusion, thermal insulation, and long-term durability.

Polycarbonate for Agriculture

Multiwall polycarbonate sheets are the ideal glazing material for greenhouses and agricultural structures. Their unique multi-layer design provides exceptional thermal insulation, reducing heating costs by up to 50% compared to single-layer glass or film.

The light-diffusing properties of opal and clear multiwall sheets create uniform light distribution inside the greenhouse, eliminating hot spots and shadows. This promotes even plant growth and higher crop yields.

With UV protection, hail resistance, and a lifespan exceeding 10 years, our polycarbonate sheets deliver outstanding return on investment for commercial and hobby growers alike.

Why Polycarbonate for Agriculture?

Light Diffusion

Multiwall structure diffuses sunlight evenly throughout the greenhouse, eliminating shadows and hot spots for uniform plant growth.

Thermal Insulation

Multi-layer air chambers reduce heat loss by up to 50% compared to glass, significantly cutting heating costs during cold months.

Hail Resistant

Virtually unbreakable polycarbonate withstands hailstorms that would shatter glass, protecting your crops and investment year after year.

UV Protection

Co-extruded UV layer blocks harmful radiation while allowing beneficial light wavelengths through for healthy plant growth.

Lightweight

8x lighter than glass — reduces structural framework costs and allows larger spans with simpler, more economical support structures.

10+ Year Lifespan

UV-protected sheets maintain clarity and performance for over 10 years, delivering excellent return on investment for growers.
