Polycarbonate for Industrial Safety

Solid polycarbonate sheets are the material of choice for industrial safety applications. At 250 times stronger than glass and virtually unbreakable, they provide maximum protection for workers and equipment without compromising visibility.

Unlike glass or acrylic, polycarbonate does not shatter or splinter on impact, eliminating the risk of secondary injuries from flying fragments. This makes it ideal for machine guards, protective barriers, and safety glazing in demanding industrial environments.

Our sheets maintain their protective properties across a wide temperature range (-40В°C to +120В°C) and offer excellent resistance to chemicals, oils, and industrial cleaning agents.

Application Areas

Critical safety applications where polycarbonate excels

Machine Guards

Protective covers for industrial machinery that shield operators from moving parts, sparks, and debris while maintaining full visibility of the production process.

Protective Screens

Safety partitions and splash guards for welding stations, grinding areas, and chemical handling zones. Non-shattering protection that won't create dangerous fragments.

Noise Barriers

Sound insulation panels for highways, railways, and industrial zones. Reduce noise by up to 35 dB while maintaining transparency and aesthetic appeal.

Security Glazing

Anti-vandal and anti-burglary glazing for banks, shops, public transport, and government buildings. Impact and puncture resistant without re-glazing costs.

Safety Partitions

Internal safety barriers in factories, warehouses, and logistics centers. Transparent partitions that maintain sightlines while separating hazardous areas.

Industrial Skylights

Durable roofing panels for factories and warehouses that provide natural daylighting while withstanding industrial environments and heavy weather.
