Polycarbonate for Signage & Design

Polycarbonate is a premium material for modern signage and design applications. With up to 90% light transmission and excellent diffusion properties, it delivers uniform and intense illumination.

Unlike acrylic, polycarbonate does not shatter and is resistant to vandalism, making it ideal for outdoor and public installations. UV protection prevents yellowing and hazing over time.

Available in a wide range of colors, tints, and transparency levels, it allows designers to create precise lighting and ambient effects with high structural durability.

Application Areas

Illuminated Signs

Raised letters, light boxes, and backlit boards enable uniform light distribution. They eliminate bright spots from LED lighting and provide a clean, even, professional appearance in any lighting conditions.

Light Diffusers

Opal and matte sheets that distribute light evenly, ideal for LED panels, ceiling lighting, and decorative lighting elements.

Backlit Panels

Menu boards, display panels, and illuminated advertising boxes designed for retail outlets, hospitality venues, and transportation terminals.

Interior Design

Translucent partitions, decorative wall panels, illuminated ceilings, and feature walls that combine aesthetics with functionality.

Outdoor Displays

Weather- and vandal-resistant display cases, information boards, and wayfinding signs for public spaces, parks, and roads.

Architectural Features

Domes, skylights, decorative canopies, and building elements where polycarbonate's formability and wide color range enable unique design solutions.

Why Polycarbonate for Signage & Design?

Premium Light Diffusion

Even light scattering ensures uniform illumination.

Impact Resistant

Up to 250 times more resistant than glass - outdoor signs and displays withstand impacts without cracking or shattering, avoiding frequent and costly replacements.

UV-Stable Colors

UV protection prevents yellowing and loss of gloss, preserving color intensity and brightness of signs during long-term outdoor use.

Design Flexibility

Easy to thermoform, cold-bend, cut, and print on. Available in a wide range of colors and translucency levels, offering broad possibilities for creative and functional solutions.

Weather Resistant

Withstands rain, wind, hail, and extreme temperatures (-40°C to +120°C) without warping, cracking, or loss of optical properties.

Thermal Stability

Maintains stability even at the elevated temperatures generated by LED lighting systems, preventing deformations and ensuring long-lasting sign quality.
