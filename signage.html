Polycarbonate for Signage & Design

Polycarbonate is the premium material for modern signage and design applications. With up to 90% light transmission and excellent light diffusion properties, it creates uniform, vibrant illumination that enhances visibility and brand impact.

Unlike acrylic, polycarbonate resists shattering and withstands vandalism, making it ideal for outdoor and public-facing installations. UV protection prevents yellowing and hazing, preserving color accuracy and brightness for years.

Available in a wide range of colors, tints, and translucency levels, our sheets enable designers to create precise atmospheric effects while maintaining the structural durability required for professional-grade signage and architectural features.

Why Polycarbonate for Signage & Design?

Superior Light Diffusion

Even light distribution eliminates LED hot spots, creating uniform illumination across sign faces and light panels for professional, high-quality results.

Vandal Resistant

250x stronger than glass — outdoor signs and displays withstand impacts and attempted vandalism without shattering, eliminating costly replacements.

UV Stable Colors

UV protection prevents yellowing and hazing, preserving color accuracy and brightness of illuminated signs for years of outdoor exposure.

Design Flexibility

Easy to thermoform, cold-bend, cut, and print on. Wide range of colors and translucency levels enable unlimited creative possibilities.

Weather Resistant

Withstands rain, wind, hail, and extreme temperatures (-40°C to +120°C) without warping, cracking, or loss of optical properties.

Thermal Stability

Maintains dimensional precision despite heat from LED lighting systems, preventing warping and ensuring consistent sign quality over time.
