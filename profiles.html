Profiles & Thermal Washers

Complete system of H-profiles, U-profiles, and installation accessories for professional polycarbonate sheet mounting.

4-20mm Compatible Sheets
100% Polycarbonate
UV Protected

Product Overview

Our profiles and accessories are designed for professional installation of multiwall and solid polycarbonate sheets. Made from 100% virgin polycarbonate with UV protection, they ensure perfect compatibility and long-lasting performance.

H-profiles connect sheets side by side, providing structural support and a clean finished look. U-profiles seal the open ends of multiwall sheets, preventing dust, moisture, and insects from entering the chambers.

Available in multiple colors including clear, bronze, blue, and brown. All profiles are compatible with our full range of sheet thicknesses from 4mm to 20mm.
