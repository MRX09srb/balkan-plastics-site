Construction & Architecture

Polycarbonate solutions that combine natural daylighting with superior thermal performance, impact resistance, and modern aesthetics for any building project.

Polycarbonate in Construction

Natural daylighting has been proven to improve well-being, increase productivity, and reduce energy costs. Our polycarbonate sheets provide the perfect balance of light transmission, thermal insulation, and structural strength for modern construction.

Whether used for roofing, skylights, facades, or interior partitions, our multiwall and solid sheets deliver significant energy savings while creating bright, comfortable indoor environments.

With U-values as low as 1.2 W/m²K and light transmission up to 90%, our products meet the demands of energy-efficient building design while maintaining architectural flexibility.

Application Areas

Our polycarbonate sheets are used across a wide range of construction projects

Roofing & Skylights

Multiwall sheets provide excellent thermal insulation with natural daylight for commercial and residential roofing. Skylights can reduce lighting energy use by up to 80%.

Facades & Cladding

Translucent polycarbonate wall panels create striking facades that diffuse natural light while providing privacy and thermal performance for modern buildings.

Canopies & Carports

Lightweight yet strong polycarbonate panels are ideal for covered parking, entrance canopies, walkway covers, and outdoor shelters that require weather protection with daylight.

Interior Partitions

Solid and multiwall sheets serve as elegant interior dividers in offices, retail spaces, and public buildings — allowing light flow while maintaining visual separation.

Swimming Pool Covers

High thermal insulation multiwall sheets are perfect for pool enclosures, extending the swimming season while reducing heating costs significantly.

Noise Barriers

Solid polycarbonate sheets provide effective sound insulation for highways, railways, and industrial zones while maintaining transparency and light transmission.

Why Polycarbonate for Construction?

Natural Daylighting

Up to 90% light transmission brings natural daylight indoors, improving well-being and reducing energy costs for artificial lighting.

Energy Efficiency

Multiwall sheets with U-values as low as 1.2 W/m²K provide superior thermal insulation, reducing heating and cooling costs year-round.

Impact Resistant

250x stronger than glass — withstands hail, extreme weather, and accidental impacts without shattering, ensuring long-term safety.

Lightweight

Up to 8x lighter than glass, reducing structural support requirements and transportation costs while enabling larger span designs.

Fire Safety

EuroClass B-s1,d0 rated — self-extinguishing polycarbonate meets strict building fire safety requirements for public and commercial structures.

Design Freedom

Available in multiple colors, finishes, and thicknesses. Cold-bendable for curved designs. Perfect for creative architectural solutions.
