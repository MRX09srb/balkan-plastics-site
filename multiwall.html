4-20mm Thickness Range

Product Overview

Our multiwall polycarbonate sheets feature a unique multi-layer structure that provides exceptional thermal insulation while maintaining excellent light transmission. The hollow chamber design creates air pockets that significantly reduce heat transfer, making them ideal for energy-efficient building solutions.

All sheets come with co-extruded UV protective layer on the external surface, ensuring long-term resistance to weathering and yellowing. The UV protection guarantees a 10-year warranty against yellowing and loss of light transmission.

Available in twin-wall (2-wall), triple-wall (3-wall), five-wall, and seven-wall configurations to meet various thermal insulation and structural requirements. Custom lengths up to 12 meters available on request.

Key Strengths

Why choose our multiwall polycarbonate sheets

UV & Hail Resistance

Co-extruded UV protective layer ensures long-term resistance to weathering, yellowing, and hail damage. 10-year warranty included.

Light Transmission

High clarity with up to 82% light transmission for clear sheets. Provides abundant natural daylight while filtering harmful UV rays.

Thermal Insulation

Multi-layer chamber structure with U-values as low as 1.2 W/m²K. Significantly reduces heating and cooling costs for energy-efficient buildings.

Impact Resistance

Virtually unbreakable — 200 times stronger than glass and 8 times lighter. Ideal for areas prone to extreme weather or vandalism.

Energy Saving

Superior thermal insulation translates directly into lower energy consumption, reducing both environmental impact and operational costs.

Versatility

Suitable for flat and curved installations. Easy to cut, drill and cold-bend on site. Wide range of colors and thicknesses for any project.

Installation & Accessories

Professional installation solutions for optimal performance

H-Profiles

Aluminium and polycarbonate H-profiles for joining sheets side by side. Available for all thicknesses from 4mm to 20mm.

U-Profiles

End closure profiles that seal the open ends of multiwall sheets, preventing moisture and dust ingress into the chambers.

Fixing Washers

Special thermal washers with UV-resistant gaskets ensure watertight fixing points while allowing for thermal expansion.
