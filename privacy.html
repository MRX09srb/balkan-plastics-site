Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Balkan Plastics Company ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide on this website (balkanplastics.com).

1. Data Controller

Balkan Plastics Company
Production: Magnetna polja BB, 24000 Subotica, Serbia
Office: Belgrade, Serbia
Email: info@balkanplastics.com

For data protection inquiries, contact us at the email above.

2. What Data We Collect

Through the contact form

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your company name (optional)
  • Your message subject and content
  • Your IP address (for spam prevention)

Automatically

  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps), retained for approximately 30 days
  • A "language" preference stored locally in your browser (does not contain personal data)

3. Why We Collect This Data

  • To respond to your inquiry — main purpose of the contact form
  • To prevent spam and abuse — IP-based rate limiting and bot detection
  • To maintain a working website — server logs help us diagnose problems

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process this data based on:

  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — responding to business inquiries from you
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — implicit when you submit the contact form

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Contact form messages: up to 3 years for business correspondence
  • Server logs: approximately 30 days
  • Email correspondence: as long as our business relationship continues, plus a reasonable archive period for record-keeping

6. Third Parties That Process Your Data

To provide this website and its features, we use the following services. These are independent companies with their own privacy practices:

  • Cloudflare, Inc. (USA) — Turnstile bot protection on the contact form. Limited behavioral data is sent to verify you are not a bot. Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Google LLC (USA) — Google Fonts CDN delivers website fonts. Your IP address is visible to Google when fonts are loaded. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Namecheap Private Email — hosts our email service. Your contact form messages are sent through and stored on their servers.
  • Namecheap VPS — hosts the server that runs our website.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tools.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / Serbian Personal Data Protection Law)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., the Serbian Commissioner for Information of Public Importance and Personal Data Protection, poverenik.rs)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@balkanplastics.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses your browser's localStorage to remember your language preference (EN or SR). This is not a cookie and is not used for tracking.

This website does not set analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any tracking technologies.

9. Data Security

We apply industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.2+) for all communication with the site
  • Firewall and intrusion prevention (fail2ban)
  • Encrypted server backups
  • Limited access to email and server systems

10. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers (Cloudflare, Google) are based outside the European Economic Area. They process data under safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission. Our own servers are located in the European Union.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email info@balkanplastics.com.
